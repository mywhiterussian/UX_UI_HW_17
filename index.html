ABOUT ME

When working on UI/UX projects, I enjoy finding beauty in simplicity: from straightforward navigation to clean design. My background lies in marketing, public relations, and project management. I managed complex projects in award-winning design agencies, and it helped me to better understand the design process and establish effective collaboration between cross-functional teams.

I am also an experienced photographer specializing in modeling/acting portfolios, portrait photography, and not-so-corporate headshots. With this experience, I am always very particular in imagery choices to support the design’s narrative. I can also take pictures of your team and add them to your website!
